Weekend Trip to Japan

I studied abroad in Taiwan fall semester 2018, and during that semester I was also able to travel to Japan!
Over a long weekend, my friends and I visited Osaka, Kyoto, and Nara.
It was an amazing trip, and has become the most watched video on my YouTube channel with over 8.5K views.

One Second a Day 2018

I decided to document 2018 by filming one second of every day.
I compiled all of those seconds into a six and a half minute video that captures so much more!
As someone with pretty bad memory, this video has had the interesting effect of helping me remember more.
Re-watching the video I can remember each day.

Adoption Home Video

This video has a special place in my heart.
When my parents went to China to adopt me, they filmed the trip.
That footage sat untouched for 20 years.
We finally got the tapes transfered into digital files, and I started editing.

Of course, I cried a lot while editing and watching this video.
It was really weird seeing myself as a baby (and my parents 20 years younger).
